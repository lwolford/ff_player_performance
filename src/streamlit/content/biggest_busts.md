The 4 most disappointing individual weekly performances of the 2025 season — ranked by how far a player's worst game fell below their average without it.

**How it's calculated:** Each player's lowest-scoring active game is identified (games where they had zero attempts, carries, targets, or kicks are excluded). Their average across all other active games serves as the baseline. Players are ranked by how far their worst game fell below that baseline. Requires 10+ active games played.
