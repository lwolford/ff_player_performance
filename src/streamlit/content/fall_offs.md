Players with the largest average points decline from the first half (Weeks 1–8) to the second half (Weeks 9–17) of the 2025 season.

**How it's calculated:** Per-game PPR averages are calculated separately for each half of the season. Players are ranked by `first half avg − second half avg`. Same eligibility criteria as Late Bloomers: at least 3 games per half and 14 total games.
