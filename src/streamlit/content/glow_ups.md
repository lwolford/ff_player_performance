Players with the greatest average points improvement from the first half (Weeks 1–8) to the second half (Weeks 9–17) of the 2025 season.

**How it's calculated:** Per-game PPR averages are calculated separately for each half of the season. Players are ranked by `second half avg − first half avg`. Requires at least 3 games played in each half and 14 total games, to filter out players with too small a sample.
