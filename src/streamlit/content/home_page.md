# 🏆 2025 NFL Fantasy Football Performance Dashboard

Welcome to an exploratory deep-dive into the 2025 NFL fantasy football season.
Every tab is designed as an **award ceremony moment**, surfacing the players and
defenses that defined the season through stats, streaks, and standout games.

Each category features a **top-3 podium** along with an **honorable mention** for
the players who were just outside greatness. Use the sidebar to navigate between
categories, filter by position, and discover the stories the box scores don't always tell.

---

## 📋 About the Data

All fantasy scoring follows **PPR (Points Per Reception)** rules, covering the
**regular season only (Weeks 1–17)**. Week 18 is excluded since most starters sit
and the results are rarely representative of true player performance.

**Fantasy Playoffs** in this dashboard are defined as **Weeks 15–17**, the stretch
that decides **true** championships.

---

## 🚧 What Could Be Better

This project has room to grow. A few improvements that would take it to the next level:

- 📈 **ADP data** — knowing where a player was drafted would let us measure which
  picks were steals and which were busts
- 🤕 **Injury data** — injury history and missed games would give crucial context
  to any performance dips or breakout stretches
