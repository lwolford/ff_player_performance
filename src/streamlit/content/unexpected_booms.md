The 4 biggest single-game outliers of the 2025 season — ranked by how far a player's best game exceeded their average score without it.

**How it's calculated:** Each player's peak game is identified, then their average PPR score across all *other* weeks is calculated as a baseline. Players are ranked by the gap between their boom score and that baseline. To keep it meaningful, only players with 10+ games played and a baseline average under 10 pts are eligible.
